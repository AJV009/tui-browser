Android (Chrome)

Chrome on Android ignores CA certs installed in Settings. Instead, accept the cert directly:

Step 1
Tap this link: open local server
A "Your connection is not private" warning will appear
Step 2
Tap "Advanced" at the bottom
Step 3
Tap "Proceed to [IP address] (unsafe)"
This is safe — it's your own server on your local network
Step 4
You should see a JSON response — the cert is now accepted
Step 5
Come back here — the status above will turn green within seconds

iOS / iPadOS (Safari)

Install the CA certificate to the device trust store:

Step 1
Download CA certificate (open in Safari)
Step 2
Go to Settings > General > VPN & Device Management
Step 3
Tap the TUI Browser profile and tap "Install"
Step 4
Go to Settings > General > About > Certificate Trust Settings
Step 5
Enable full trust for the TUI Browser CA

Desktop

Option A
Visit local server and accept the warning
Option B
Run sudo mkcert -install on the host to trust system-wide
